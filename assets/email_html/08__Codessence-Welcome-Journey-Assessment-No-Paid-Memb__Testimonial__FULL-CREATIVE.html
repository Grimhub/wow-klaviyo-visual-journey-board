Codessence is that system. And it does not stop at the assessments and the journeys. It comes with you into daily life as a complete, coherent, deeply intelligent mentorship. A daily nudge. A daily reminder. A daily return to yourself no different than brushing your teeth. Because knowing yourself should not be a luxury reserved for retreats and big moments. Every moment of asking: is this truly where I want to go? Does this align with my desire, my heart, my truth, my essence? And if it does not, why are we pouring our time and energy in that direction?
